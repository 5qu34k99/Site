Lycoming Video Contest

Welcome! Thank you for your interest in LVC Shorts. How much information can be shared in a 60 seconds? A huge amount of information can be shared in a 60 seconds! Our goal is making Lycoming County more user friendly 60 Seconds at a time. The theme is 60 second videos of anything to do with tourism in Lycoming County.

Examples of 60 second videos: Trails, bike paths, walking tours, town parks, swimming pools, state parks, game lands, state pheasant farm, fish hatchery’s, waterfalls, docks, vistas, ccc roads, Bald Eagle viewing areas, historical markers, farmers markets, Amish goods, cage free eggs, farm tours, town tours, historical societies, bunkers, granges, campgrounds, motels/hotels, theaters’, drive ins, outfitters, coffee shops, landscaping nurseries, cemeteries, pickers, marts, malls, delis, car repair, car dealers, crafts, amusements, clubs, schools, and more.

Three judges will select winners. The timeline is to have the first contest on 1st Friday in May or June, and to continue from there. These videos will be seen by people from all over the world and all walks of life with two large screen TVs in Little League World Grand Slam Parade and driving by WVIA TV live.
