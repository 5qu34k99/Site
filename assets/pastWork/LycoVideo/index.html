Lycoming Video Contest

Welcome! Thank you for your interest in LVC Shorts. How long is a minute? Of course; sixty (60) seconds, how much information can be shared in a 60 seconds? A huge amount of information can be shared in a 60 seconds. You’d be surprised how much you share in 60 seconds can be absorbed by the viewer. The goal is making Lycoming County User Friendlier 60 Seconds at a Time. The theme is 60 second videos of anything to do with tourism in Lycoming County, by promoting Lycoming County with fun things to do with a large percentage that are free. Furthermore; bringing, tourist moneys to local businesses and exposure to local talent. This will be the guide for places, activities, entertainment, accommodations, vehicle repairs, and encouragement of excellence of the videographers. Examples of 60 second videos: Trails, bike paths, walking tours, town parks, swimming pools, state parks, game lands, state pheasant farm, fish hatchery’s, waterfalls, docks, vistas, ccc roads, Bald Eagle viewing areas, historical markers, farmers markets, Amish goods, cage free eggs, farm tours, town tours, historical societies, bunkers, granges, campgrounds, motels/hotels, theaters’, drive ins, outfitters, coffee shops, landscaping nurseries, cemeteries, pickers, marts, malls, delis, car repair, car dealers, crafts, amusements, clubs, PCT, Lycoming College, Newport Business school, housing, BB/BS, S.T.E.P, hospitals, doctors, dentist,…etc. Three judges will select winners. Also see Point System draft. Time line is 1st contest 1st Friday in May or June downtown Williamsport, then monthly at various locations in Lycoming County. These videos will be seen by people from all over the world and all walks of life with two large screen TVs in Little League World Grand Slam Parade, driving by WVIA TV live, and passing out round to it’s along the way. Round to it’s, will have “brand mark” with contact information. Big screen TV will have winning videos with scroll of contact information. With proper management this can be a great thing for locals, tourist, videographers, actors, and artist. Must mention this is also green you can visit Lycoming County from anyplace, anytime with little or no carbon footprint used for the viewer to view. Once videos are posted they can be viewed for every. All Churches and community leaders in Lycoming County will be featured by LVC Shorts.
