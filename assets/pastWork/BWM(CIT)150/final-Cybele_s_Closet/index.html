About Cybele's Closet


Mission

We strive to provide a unique, stylish t-shirt alternative made from sustainable, eco-friendly resources. We cater to the tastes and figures of every girl.


Story

Cybele (sib-eh-lee) is an ancient earth goddess, you may know her better as Mother Nature - Who better to represent a company that creates eco-friendly attire for all of the goddesses that inhabit the Earth! At Cybele's Closet, we believe that a company must treat its customers and the environment with respect. We make t-shirts for girls who want a break from shapeless shirts that hide their figures, and materials that are harsh on their skin and the environment. T-shirts from Cybele's Closet are created from ultra-soft sustainable, eco-friendly resources such as organic cotton, bamboo, and hemp. They have a modern, long-length and a slightly form-fitting shape to accentuate curves. The creative designs on our t-shirts are stylish, artistic, and inspired by things you love.
