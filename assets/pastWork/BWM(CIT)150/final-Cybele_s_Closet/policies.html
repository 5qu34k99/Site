Policies


Shipping Details:

All orders ship via USPS. Prices calculated based upon USPS rates and your location.


Return Policy:

We always want you to be happy with your shopping experience at Cybele's Closet! To help make this a reality, you may return any unwashed t-shirt within 90 days of purchase for a full refund (including shipping both ways), no questions asked.


Privacy Policy:

The last thing we want to do is violate your privacy, so we keep your info private! We never share or sell any of your information and promise to always keep it secure. Any information you provide will only be used to process your order and/or to help make your shopping experience at Cybele's Closet the best it can be.


Green Statement:

Cybele's Closet promises to be kind to the Earth. It doesn't make sense to destroy the place you live and we all live on the Earth, so we must do our part to protect our home. We only buy from eco-friendly, sustainable, fair-trade suppliers and we give back whenever possible. Our charitable donations have no cap, thus there is no limit on funds raised for charities. We are happy to provide financial statements of our charitable donations upon request.
